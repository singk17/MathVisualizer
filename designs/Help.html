How to use the Linear Transformation
1. Click on the Start Graphing button to launch the matrix visualizer.
2. Use the + button to add a matrix/vector. You may click this button to add as many matrices and vectors you would like to visualize at a time. Use the x to delete matrices and vectors.
3. Click on the blank boxes in the matrices and vectors to type in the desired values. You can also click the up and down arrows.
4. For help with transformations, click on the ? on the side to access transformation
5. Use on the + button to zoom in on the graph and - button to zoom out.
6. Use the - button to minimize the icons on the side.
7. Use the < button to maximize the icons on the side.
8. Use the # button to make the grid appear or disappear.
9. Use the HOME button to navigate to the home page.
