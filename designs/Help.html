How to use the Linear Transformation
1. Click on the Start Graphing button to launch the matrix visualizer.
2. Click the + button to add a matrix/vector. You may click this button to add as many matrices and vectors you would like to visualize at a time.
3. Click on the blank boxes in the matrices and vectors to type in the desired values. You can also click the up and down arrows.
4. For help with transformations, click on the ? on the side to access transformation
5. Click on the + button to zoom in on the graph and - button to zoom out.
